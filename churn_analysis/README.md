# Customer Churn Analysis

## Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer churn behavior to understand why customers leave and what factors influence retention.  
The analysis was performed using **Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)**.

---

## Objectives
- Identify churned vs retained customers
- Calculate churn rate
- Understand behavioral patterns behind churn
- Generate actionable business insights

---

## Tools & Technologies
- Python
- Pandas (data manipulation)
- Matplotlib / Seaborn (visualization)
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## Key Metric
- **Churn Rate: ~17.7%**
- Retention Rate: ~82%

---

## Analysis Performed
- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Comparison between churned and retained customers
- Visualization of customer behavior patterns
- Identification of potential churn drivers

---

## Key Insights
- Most customers are retained, but a significant portion still churns
- Certain behavioral patterns may indicate higher churn risk
- Customer engagement is a key factor in retention

---

## Business Impact
This analysis helps businesses:
- Improve customer retention strategies
- Identify at-risk customers early
- Optimize marketing and engagement efforts
